- Overview -

Most of the products sold on Wish are shipped from China, and the prolonged shipping time and high cost have almost been an outstanding issue and the top one reason that customers leave our platform. So we want to improve shipping experience by offering flat shipping and free shipping over a certain amount of value.

The flat shipping promotion was a success, but GMV flattened out after the first three days of the promo. The assumption is that the message was not prominent enough in the app. As a result the product team has decided to make it a feature and surface the information in product details, feed and cart.
